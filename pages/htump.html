Connecting via metamask


The approvement window should pop up automatically. If it didnt happen, press the "connect" button in the upper right corner.

Getting a mining card

Using mining platform requires you to have a mining card wich are available on the exchange. There is also an exchange on the mining platform. Celect a preferable mining card and press buy.
A pop up metamask window should appear. Press confirm, the card you bought is now yours. If it didnt happen, make sure the transaction was successful.

Activating the card

Go to Pools and select the pool of your mining card. Your card should load within 30 seconds.
Press the pink triangle next to your miner. Confirm the transaction and voila! Your card is now activated! To deactivate the card do the same thing.
If a user holds an ArtOnline NFT Graphics Card, and that card is activated, it will continue to provide passive returns until the card is de-activated. After de-activation the user has the option to sell at premium prices, sell for less on the exchange or, (coming soon) auction the NFT to the highest bidder.

Spinner

A spinner can be activated on each nft gpu, It increases mining by 1% and costs 3500 art to activate. In a pool of 3 GPUs you can activate up to 3 spinners for a total of 3% mining bonus on the pool. So if you have 5 modules you can only activate 5 spinners.

Claiming rewards

To claim the rewards go to the mining pool and press "Get reward" button.
These 2 buttons will pop up. Claim now (20% tax) means 20% of your mined artonline will get deducted (15% burned and 5% credited to the staking pool), but you get the reward right after transaction. So if you mined 10000 artonline you only get 8000.
Stake for 6 months means you wont be able to use your artonline for 6 months, but no tax.

Using calculator

Calculator shows how much artonline will be mined in selected amount of time. Select your pool in "GPU" field. Enter current pool size for selected miner in "miners" field. Enter amount of your miners in "GPU'S" field. You get the result in Art/min.
